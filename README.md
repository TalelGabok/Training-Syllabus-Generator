# Training Syllabus Generator

AI-powered tool for generating customized training syllabi based on client intake information. Built for manufacturing excellence training consultants.

## üåü Features

- **Comprehensive Intake Form** - Capture all client details, training requirements, and context
- **AI-Powered Generation** - Uses OpenAI GPT-4 or Anthropic Claude to create detailed syllabi
- **Dual API Support** - Choose between OpenAI or Anthropic based on your preference
- **Professional Output** - Download syllabi as JSON or HTML (opens in Microsoft Word)
- **Manufacturing Focus** - Pre-configured with manufacturing training domains and expertise areas
- **No Backend Required** - Runs entirely in the browser

## üöÄ Quick Start

### Live Site

Visit: `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/REPO-NAME`

### Getting Started

1. **Get an API Key** from either:
   - [OpenAI](https://platform.openai.com/api-keys)
   - [Anthropic](https://console.anthropic.com/)

2. **Open the app** and enter your API key in the configuration section

3. **Fill out the intake form** with client information:
   - Company basics
   - Training libraries needed
   - Business goals and pain points
   - Target audience
   - Additional context

4. **Generate** your customized syllabus

5. **Download** in your preferred format (JSON or HTML)

## üìã How to Use

### Step 1: API Configuration
- Select your AI provider (OpenAI or Anthropic)
- Enter your API key
- The key is stored only for your current session

### Step 2: Fill Out Intake Form

**Section A: Company Information**
- Company name, website, industry
- Contact person details
- Company size and location

**Section B: Training Intent**
- Select training libraries (check all that apply):
  - Leadership & Supervisory
  - Operational Excellence & Lean/CI
  - Quality Systems & Compliance
  - Change Leadership & Culture
  - Safety & Operational Risk
  - Supply Chain & Lean Logistics
  - Metrics & Data-Driven Decisions
  - Soft Skills for Operations
- Business goals
- Pain points
- Target audience
- Delivery preference (in-person/remote/blended)

**Section C: Additional Context** (Optional but helpful)
- Business goals details
- Specific pain points
- Any other relevant information

### Step 3: Generate & Download
- Click "Generate Syllabus"
- Review the generated content
- Download as JSON or HTML

## üéØ Training Domains

The generator understands these manufacturing training domains:

- **Metrics & Data**: Data literacy, KPI definition, dashboards, SPC, control charts
- **Soft Skills**: Communication, influencing, time management, conflict resolution

## üí° Best Practices

### For Better Results:
1. **Be specific** - The more detail you provide, the better the output
2. **Include context** - Business goals and pain points help tailor the content
3. **Select relevant libraries** - Choose only the training areas that apply
4. **Review and customize** - AI-generated content should be reviewed and adapted
5. **Save your work** - Download both JSON and HTML for your records

### Cost Management:
- Each generation uses API tokens (costs money)
- Be thoughtful about regenerating
- Review your API provider's pricing
- Consider setting usage limits in your API dashboard
